History of Animation

Animation has evolved significantly, from flip books and hand-drawn sequences to CGI-powered films. Pioneers like Walt Disney inspired modern animation. Today, CGI has made animation more realistic, though it’s often viewed as a genre for children. Movies like "Coraline" (2009) and "Spirited Away" (2002) have helped bridge this perception gap.

Animation is now vital in film, gaming, advertising, and more, with tools like Toon Boom Harmony, Procreate, and Blender allowing solo projects, exemplified by creators like Olof Storm.
